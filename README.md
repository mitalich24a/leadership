You are paid to deliver value — meeting expectations is the baseline.

Growth is not optional. If you are doing the same as yesterday, you are already falling behind.

Your performance is reflected in:

* your project outcomes
* your team’s effectiveness
* your communication and collaboration

Your growth is tied to:

* how your team grows
* how your project scales
* how well you enable others

Progress has levels:

* Same as yesterday → decline
* Slightly better → keeping pace
* Clearly better → expected progression
* **Consistently exceptional → real growth**

      This is where differentiation happens.
      This is where you stand out, get noticed, and accelerate.
      Everything below this is just the baseline.
